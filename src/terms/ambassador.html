Ambassadors Program

Welcome to the YellowOracle.com Ambassadors Program! We appreciate your interest in becoming an Ambassador and helping us grow.

Eligibility and Enrollment

To become a YellowOracle.com Ambassador, please review the eligibility criteria below and complete the enrollment process.

  • Eligibility: Ambassadors must be 18 years or older and have the authority to act on behalf of any organization they represent.
  • Enrollment: To become an Ambassador, eligible individuals or entities must register through YellowOracle.com's official process, receiving a unique Ambassador ID upon approval.

Residual Income and Payments

As a YellowOracle.com Ambassador, you have the opportunity to earn a lifetime residual income. Here are the details:

  • Residual Income: Ambassadors earn a 15% commission on fees associated with purchases of services from users who utilize their unique Ambassador ID.
  • Earnings: Earnings are calculated monthly, and there is a minimum payout threshold of $100 USD. If the threshold is not met, earnings will roll over to the next month.
  • Payments: Payments are disbursed monthly to Ambassadors' designated accounts, subject to the terms and conditions of the payment processor used by YellowOracle.com.

Responsibilities and Conduct

As an Ambassador, you play a crucial role in promoting YellowOracle.com services while maintaining high standards of conduct:

  • Promotion: Ambassadors are responsible for promoting YellowOracle.com services to end-users and employers while utilizing their unique Ambassador ID.
  • Ethical Standards: All promotional methods must comply with applicable laws, regulations, and ethical standards. YellowOracle.com reserves the right to review and approve or reject any promotional materials or methods.
  • Professionalism: Ambassadors should maintain the highest standards of integrity and professionalism, reflecting positively on the YellowOracle.com brand.

Feedback and Continuous Improvement

We value your insights and encourage open communication for program enhancement:

  • Feedback: YellowOracle.com values Ambassadors' insights and encourages the submission of comments and suggestions to enhance the user and employer experience.
  • Continuous Dialogue: Continuous dialogue is encouraged to ensure the program remains effective and mutually beneficial.

Term and Termination

The Ambassador relationship is considered active until terminated by either party:

  • Termination: Either party may terminate the agreement with written notice. Upon termination, the Ambassador will receive any last payments owed and will no longer accrue new income from the program.

Miscellaneous

Please review the following miscellaneous terms:

  • Policy Changes: YellowOracle.com reserves the right to modify these terms at any time. Continued participation in the Ambassador program constitutes acceptance of these changes.
  • Disputes: Disputes arising under these terms will be resolved according to the laws and courts of the jurisdiction in which YellowOracle.com is based.
