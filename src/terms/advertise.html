Advertisers Program

Welcome to the YellowOracle.com Advertisers Program! We offer a unique opportunity for employers, universities, and trade schools to connect with potential candidates through targeted advertising powered by our AI-driven platform.

Eligibility and Enrollment

To participate in our Advertisers Program, please review the eligibility criteria below and complete the enrollment process.

  • Eligibility: Advertisers must have the authority to represent the entity they are advertising for and agree to our advertising policies.
  • Enrollment: Entities must complete the official registration process on YellowOracle.com, agreeing to the terms and conditions of our advertising program.

Billing and Payments

Our flexible billing options are designed to accommodate your advertising budget and goals.

  • Pay-Per-Click (PPC): Advertisers are billed based on the number of clicks their ads receive.
  • Impressions: Choose to be billed based on the number of times your ad is displayed, regardless of clicks.
  • Payments: Billing occurs monthly, with detailed reports provided to track ad performance and expenditures.

Advertising Policies and Conduct

To ensure the success and integrity of our advertising platform, we require all advertisers to adhere to our policies:

  • Content Standards: All advertisements must meet our content standards, avoiding prohibited content and ensuring accuracy.
  • Legal Compliance: Advertisements must comply with all applicable laws, including those related to advertising and privacy.
  • Quality and Relevance: We reserve the right to review and approve ads to ensure they are relevant and of high quality, aligning with our users' interests and career aspirations.

Performance Tracking and Support

YellowOracle.com provides robust tools and support to help maximize the impact of your advertising campaigns:

  • Analytics: Access detailed analytics to track ad performance, user engagement, and ROI.
  • Support: Our dedicated support team is available to assist with campaign setup, optimization, and any questions you may have.

Term and Termination

The terms of our advertising agreement are designed to be flexible, recognizing the evolving needs of our advertisers:

  • Termination: Advertisers may terminate their participation in the program with notice, subject to the terms of the agreement.

Miscellaneous

Important additional terms:

  • Changes to Terms: YellowOracle.com reserves the right to modify the terms of the Advertisers Program at any time. Continued participation constitutes acceptance of these changes.
  • Dispute Resolution: Any disputes related to advertising will be resolved according to the laws of the jurisdiction where YellowOracle.com operates.
